MAYFAIR FIT OUT FOR KINGSLEY CAPITAL PARTNERS

It gives us great pleasure to announce that we have recently been appointed to carry out an office fit out project in Mayfair for Kingsley Capital Partners. KCP is a private equity and venture capital firm whose investors are global family offices and institutional investors. We worked with KCP over many months and assisted the client with the building selection process. KCP have now found the perfect space for their business needs, and we look forward to starting fit out works in the very near future.





GLOBAL COMMODITY INDUSTRY LEADER CHOOSES NICHE, AGAIN

The team at Niche Projects are excited to announce that we have been appointed by Javelin, global commodities, to do their new offices. A few years back, Niche were in charge of the design and build of their current offices, and with their expanding numbers, Javelin are moving to a larger space.





NEW FIT OUT PROJECT WITH SPECIALIST LENDER

We are delighted to announce that we were successful in winning an office fit out project with Oblix Capital. Oblix Capital a specialist lender that provides bespoke, non-regulated shirt term finance for the UK property market has chosen to partner with Niche for its upcoming office relocation to Holborn. The new space will require a fit out for a reception area, a number of meeting rooms, private offices and a breakout space.





NEW CITY OFFICE DESIGN & FIT OUT PROJECT

Niche Projects has been appointed to carry out the design and fit out of Allenby Capital's new office space, located in the City of London. The design will incorporate classic materials, adhering to the company culture. The pioneering finance company focuses on advising and assisting growing businesses, both in the public and private sector, offering a range of advisory services to companies.





PROFESSIONAL ADVISORS

Niche Projects has been appointed to manage the fit out and design for DV Advisory Group's new office, located in Central London. The company is an independent and entrepreneurial provider of capital, focusing on investment in early stage fast-growing global companies.
